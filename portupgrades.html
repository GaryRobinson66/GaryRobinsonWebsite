Port Upgrades

Benefits of Construction Project

  • Increased connectivity within Europe
  • Increased connectivity with the rest of Ireland
  • 5-fold increase in ships transiting through Rosslare
  • Improved facilities with passengers
  • New digitaized Check-in system
  • New port access road direct connected to the M11
  • Offshore renewable energy plant
  • Sufficient reduction of traffic clash points within the port
  • Increased efficient in loading discharging loading operations by streamlining traffic flow in the port

Viability of Construction Project

A large proportion of this phase of the construction can be developed without any impact on the existing port operations as the construction is on the area adjoining the port facility. Phase one would enable freight to access the port along the new road and roundabout while check in at the new entry location. Port facilities are to be upgraded as part of government plans to meet customs, revenue, and agriculture and immigration controls post-Brexit with significant investment by Government. This investment along with the Rosslare Europort Masterplan will ensure that the port is Brexit ready and has the required facilities to meet future growth.
