Passenger Ferries

Rosslare Europort services many different routes across the United Kingdom and Europe. Passenger ferries operate to and from Fishguard with Stena Line. Irish Ferries operate the service to Pembroke Dock. Cherbourg is reached through both Stena Line and Brittany Ferries. Brittany Ferries also operate from Rosslare to Bilbao in Spain. DFDS operates a service from Rosslare to Dunkirk with six sailings a week in each direction. Irish Ferries has introduced a twice-daily ship on the Rosslare to Pembroke route. It is the fastest RORO vessel between Ireland and the UK, with capacity for 1,500 passengers, 100 freight vehicles and 700 cars. A freight route between Rosslare and Zeebrugge, Belgium was introduced by Finnlines, part of the Grimaldi Group, for a twice weekly ro-ro service between the two ports. Neptune lines also include Rosslare in a loop service which includes Santander, Le Havre, Southampton and Zeebrugge, which is used to import motor vehicles.
